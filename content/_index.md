# The Thread

How stable patterns become meaning. And how to weave them consciously.

<a id="the-thread"></a>
## What Is The Thread?

The Thread is a cross-scale lens for seeing how meaning forms and endures.

1. Parts
2. Repeated Interaction
3. Protective Container
4. Stable Pattern
5. New Higher-Level Part

<a id="weavers-way"></a>
## Weaver's Way

A practical, diagnostic way to notice recurring patterns, evaluate whether they sustain life, and adjust containers over time.
